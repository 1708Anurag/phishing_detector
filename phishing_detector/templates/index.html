What it checks

Rule-based heuristics

Flags urgency language, credential requests, suspicious domains, IP-based links, brand impersonation, and lookalike TLDs commonly used in phishing campaigns.

Machine learning classifier

A TF-IDF + Logistic Regression model trained on labeled phishing/legitimate email samples estimates the probability that the wording itself matches known phishing patterns.

Scan history & dashboard

Every scan is saved to a database per user, so your team can review past results, track trends, and demonstrate the system during evaluation.

Explainable results

Every verdict comes with a plain-English list of exactly which signals triggered it — ideal for a project demo or viva.
